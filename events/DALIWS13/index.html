NSF DALI Workshop on Dynamic Languages for Scalable Data Analytics

November 1st, 2013@SPLASH in Indianapolis, IN

The DALI workshop is a one-day, by-invitation, workshop dedicated to exploring needs of scientific communities for new languages and infrastructures for the scalable analysis of data. Languages such as R, Matlab, Python and Julia, are of particular interest but the scope of discussions includes all languages and infrastructures used for data analytics.

The workshop will consist of a number of short talks and discussions. Part of the output of the workshop will be a recommendation to the National Science Foundation for funding an institute to support the development of open infrastructures for scalable data analytics. The workshop will bring together practitioners and language developers from academia and industry.

Funded by the NSF Software Infrastructure for Sustained Innovation (SI2) program.
