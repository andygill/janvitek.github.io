Spas

Bagni di Bormio    SPA/Old Baths

Step back in time to the characteristic and evocative Ancient Roman Baths. A dive into more than two thousand years of history, from the Roman baths, the natural warm caves to the panoramic open air swimming pool overlooking the Bormio valley. More than 30 different thermal services are included with the entrance ticket to the Bagni Vecchi: external panoramic swimming pool, old rock cave, hydro massage bathtubs, saunas, steam rooms...

Parco dello Stelvio

(From www.parks.it)
Parco Nazionale dello Stelvio is the largest not only among the Italian parks, but also in the whole Alpine chain. It stretches in the heart of the Central Alps and includes typical valleys shaped by the action of ice and water flowing down from the imposing massif of the Ortles-Cevedale group. Majestic ridges, luxuriant forests, high mountain green grasslands crossed by foaming streams springing from perennial glaciers form the landscape of the Park. Many rare animal and plant species live in wide ecosystems present in territory thanks to its remarkable differences in height and to its morphological variety. The landscape is scattered with villages and "masi" at the bottom of the valleys and on the mountain slopes: they are charming evidences of rural and religious architecture which complete the scenery in harmony with the surrounding environment.
