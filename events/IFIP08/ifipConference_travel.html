By bus organized by the WG2.4

  • We will organize a bus from Malpensa at 14:30. The price will be 40 euros.

By Public Transportation

  • Take a shuttle bus from Malpensa Airport to Milano Central Station either by Malpensa Bus Express, Malpensa Express (departures and arrivals), or Malpensa Shuttle ;
  • then take a train from Milano Central Station to Tirano;
  • finally a bus from Tirano to Bormio;
  • Once in Bormio, you can get a local Bus at Tirano-Bormio bus stop and then reach your Hotel and the conference center.  

By Car

  • From Malpensa take highway A8 towards Milano;
  • then highway A4 towards Venezia (note: high traffic on Friday from 4pm on and on Sun from 4pm on) ;
  • take exit "Cinisello B. - Sesto San Giovanni - Viale Zara";
  • at the roundabout take a LEFT at the first exit of the roundabout, go straight for 300 mt;
  • then turn RIGHT at 90 degrees and follow direction Monza-Lecco and then Lecco-Erba;
  • Once you get to Lecco follow towards Colico - Morbegno - Sondrio - Tirano - Bormio. In general you might find the indication "Valtellina" which is the name of the valley where all these cities are located, at the end of wich you will find Bormio.
