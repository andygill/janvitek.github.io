REGISTRATION

 

The school and lectures will be held at the Majestic Plaza Hotel in Prague. Registration is all inclusive, 6 nights at the Plaza, breakfast, lunch and breaks as well as a conference dinner and a cultural outing.


Full registration is €900, with €100 additional fee for a single room. The price of the school without accommodation is €500.


Thanks to generous donations from Microsoft, Intel and IBM, between 20 and 30 grants will be available.


The school is targeted at doctoral students and researchers in areas related to programming concurrent systems. Time will be allocated for student presentations. Applicants should indicate their research area in their application, preference will be given to student working in the field.


The deadline for application for a grant is March 28, 2008. There is a 40 person limit on the number of participants, late applications will be considered only if there is room.

Microsoft grants are restricted to students from Eastern Europe.
