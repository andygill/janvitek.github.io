REGISTRATION

 

The school and lectures will be held at IIIT-Bangalore in India. Registration is all inclusive, 6 nights, breakfast, and breaks as well as a conference dinner and a cultural outing.


Full registration is $900, with $100 additional fee for a single room. The price of the school without accommodation is $500.


Thanks to generous donations from Microsoft, Intel, Infosys and IBM, between 20 and 30 grants will be available. In particular we will strive to cover the full cost of the school for students who could otherwise not attend. A limited number of travel grants are available for US students.


The school is targeted at doctoral students and researchers in areas related to programming concurrent systems. Time will be allocated for student presentations. Applicants should indicate their research area in their application, preference will be given to student working in the field.


The deadline for application for a grant is March 28, 2010. There is a 40 person limit on the number of participants, late applications will be considered only if there is room.
